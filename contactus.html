Frequently Asked Questions

Agriloop connects households, businesses, and farms with an efficient waste collection and segregation system. Using real-time detection through live camera feeds, we categorize waste, coordinate timely pickups, and transform organic waste into valuable resources like compost, fertilizers, and biogas.

Agriloop serves various groups, including households, farmers, restaurants, factories, and nurseries. Whether you're a waste producer or someone looking to buy organic products, our platform facilitates easy exchanges.

Once you sign up, you can use our web app to schedule waste pickups. You can select a time slot that suits you, and Agriloop will coordinate with local municipalities to ensure timely and efficient waste collection.

Our online store offers a wide variety of organic products, including compost, fertilizers, and fresh produce like fruits, vegetables, and dairy. You can also buy gardening essentials and organic plant care products.

Agriloop follows a circular economy model where farmers can exchange their organic produce, such as fruits and vegetables, for compost, biogas, and other organic products. This creates a sustainable cycle that benefits both farmers and the environment.
