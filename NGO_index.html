How It Works

  1. NGOs collect recyclable waste from communities and businesses
  2. AgriLoop processes and transforms the waste into valuable agricultural resources
  3. Farmers receive these resources at subsidized rates, improving crop yields
  4. The cycle continues, creating a sustainable loop of waste management and agricultural support
